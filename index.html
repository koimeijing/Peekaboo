关于 Peekaboo

Peekaboo 是一款专为海外儿童沙盒游戏从业者设计的竞品内容追踪工具。你可以用它收藏竞品在各平台发布的宣传内容、自动追踪应用商店的版本更新，并通过 AI 分析读懂每一条内容背后的视觉策略、流行趋势和商业意图，帮你把散落在各处的竞品信息变成有价值的行业洞察。

Peekaboo is a competitor content tracking tool built for professionals in the kids' sandbox game space. Collect promotional content from major platforms, auto-track app store updates, and use AI analysis to decode the visual strategy, trends, and business intent behind every post — turning scattered competitor signals into actionable insights.
